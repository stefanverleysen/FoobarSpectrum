Frequently Asked Questions

Foobar Spectrum is a dynamic audio visualizer that offers real-time graphical representation of soundtracks. It helps users to visually experience their music through spectrum analysis.

The spectrum analyzer in Foobar Spectrum works by breaking down audio signals into individual frequency components to create a visual representation of the sound, enabling users to see the intricate details of their music.
